About Debapriya

Based in Kolkata, I am a passionate documentary and street photographer dedicated to capturing the authentic beauty of daily life, cultural rituals, and raw human emotion through my lens.

My work focuses on storytelling that honors the dignity of my subjects while revealing the extraordinary within the ordinary. I specialize in long exposure photography, finding poetry in the passage of time and the dance of light.

Currently pursuing a B.Sc. in Film and Television at iLEAD Kolkata, I continue to expand my understanding of visual narrative and cinematic storytelling techniques.
